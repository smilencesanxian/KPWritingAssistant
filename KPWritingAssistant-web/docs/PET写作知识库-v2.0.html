Part 3. 故事类 (Story) 按叙事结构 + 常用元素归类

故事写作的关键是"一波三折"和"细节描写"。我们把素材放进叙事流程里，让故事立刻生动起来。

结构块 功能 典型素材与例句
开头承接 承接首句，设置背景，引入人物状态。 句型: It was... when... I was... when suddenly...
时态: 过去进行时 (was/were doing)
I was waiting for the bus when I saw a tiger running towards me. Page 9

He was reading a book when an apple fell on his head. Page 11范文分析-2
发展推进 描述一系列动作，使用连接词使故事流畅。 时间词: first then later a few days later finally in the end Page 9
动作副词: Instantly Quickly Slowly Carefully
Instantly, she picked up the letter and started to read it. Page 10范文分析-1

A few days later, he went back to that cave with his friends. Page 9
高潮转折 故事发生意外转折，情绪达到顶点。 转折词: but however suddenly to one's surprise
情绪词: panicked astonished amazed exhausted
评论副词: unfortunately luckily fortunately
Suddenly, I heard a weak cry. A boy was lying under the deck! Page 12范文2

To their surprise, it was a cute little rabbit! Page 15范文1
结尾收束 总结结局，点明意义或留下回味。 结尾句: That day, we became friends... It was an incredible encounter...
升华: ...became a wonderful memory... ...added a splash of color to their ordinary lives.
That day at the beach became a wonderful memory for Jesse and her friend. Page 13范文1

This adventure had added a splash of color to their ordinary lives. Page 13范文2

通用素材库 (Tool Box)

无论写什么，这些"万能"词汇都能帮你提升语言质量。

⏱️ 亮点时间/逻辑连接词

  • First 首先
  • Then 然后
  • Later 之后
  • A few days later 几天后
  • Finally 最后
  • In the end 最终
  • However 然而
  • Suddenly 突然
  • Unfortunately 不幸的是
  • Luckily 幸运的是
  • As soon as 一...就...

🏃 高级动作副词

  • Instantly 立刻
  • Quickly 快速地
  • Slowly 缓慢地
  • Carefully 小心地
  • Angrily 生气地
  • Gracefully 优雅地
  • Leisurely 悠闲地

😊 情绪形容词

  • Excited 兴奋的
  • Amazed 惊奇的
  • Panicked 恐慌的
  • Relieved 松了一口气的
  • Astonished 震惊的
  • Exhausted 筋疲力尽的
  • Delighted 高兴的

💪 万能动词短语

  • take a leisurely walk 悠闲地散步
  • have a picnic 野餐
  • boost confidence 提升自信
  • offer to help 主动帮忙
  • give a compliment 给予赞美
  • escape from stress 逃离压力
  • unwind 放松

🏆 高分句型结构

  • 定语从句 ...which / that / who...
  • It allows me to... 这让我能够...
  • It is a wonderful idea to... ...是个好主意
  • I believe that... 我相信...
  • The reason why...is that... ...的原因是...
