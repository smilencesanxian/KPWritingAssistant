### **Part 1. 邮件类 (Email) —— 按写作结构归类**

邮件写作有固定的“社交套路”，我们把素材放进“开头-主体-结尾”的框架里，背熟就能灵活运用。

#### **开头**
| 细分功能     | 素材                                                         | 含义                        | [主题]例句（标注出素材来源）                                 |
| :----------- | :----------------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------- |
| **回应感谢** | Thank you for...                                             | 感谢...                     | **Thank you for letting us know** about the film evening this Friday! (源自2025.2.9广州邮件) |
| **表达激动** | I'm really excited / thrilled / over the moon / can't wait to... | 我很兴奋/高兴极了/等不及... | **I'm over the moon that** we're having a fun camping trip together next Friday! (源自2026.1.10广州邮件) |
| **接受邀请** | I'd love to...                                               | 我很乐意...                 | **I'd love to** make a rocket model with you! (源自2025.7.19北京邮件) |

#### **主体**
| 主题              | 素材                                                         | 含义                                           | 例句（标注出素材来源）                                       |
| :---------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------- |
| **表达喜好**      | I prefer to... <br> would be more interesting <br> is a great idea | 我更喜欢... <br> 会更有趣 <br> 是个好主意      | **I prefer to watch comedies** because they always make me laugh. (源自2025.2.9广州邮件) <br> I think **the history of computers would be more interesting** because there are many cool stories. (源自2025.8.16北京邮件) |
| **提出建议**      | How about...? <br> We could... <br> I suggest packing...     | ...怎么样？<br> 我们可以... <br> 我建议带上... | **How about having the party in the school hall?** (源自2025.6.14北京邮件) <br> **I suggest packing your favorite books**, so we can browse them. (源自2025.11.9南京邮件) |
| **解释原因**      | because... <br> so that... <br> which...                     | 因为... <br> 以便... <br> 这（定语从句）       | I prefer quiet and gentle songs **because they make me feel relaxed**. (通用素材) <br> We need a peaceful atmosphere, **so games would be too loud to disturb them**. (源自2025.11.9南京邮件) |
| **礼貌拒绝+理由** | Unfortunately, I'm not available... <br> I'm sorry to say that I won't be able to... | 不幸的是，我没空... <br> 很抱歉我不能...       | **Unfortunately, I'm not available this Wednesday** since I have already planned a family trip. (源自2025.8.16北京邮件) <br> **I'm sorry to say that I won't be able to help clean** the classroom, because I have an online math class. (源自2025.2.9广州邮件) |

#### **结尾**

### **Part 2. 文章类 (Article) —— 按主题归类**

文章类题目要求观点清晰、论证有力。我们把素材按话题打包，考到哪个话题，就调取哪个“素材包”。

| 主题                               | 核心素材（亮点词/短语/句型）                                 | 例句（标注出素材来源）                                       |
| :--------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------- |
| **地点描述** (Favorite place)      | **环境**: peaceful, beautiful, vibrant, graceful <br> **活动**: take leisurely walks, ride my bike, have picnics <br> **感受**: unwind, ideal, a perfect way to end the day | The park is **filled with** tall trees, **vibrant** flowers, and a small pond where ducks **gracefully** swim. (源自2025.1.11北京范文) <br> The beautiful view of the sky **offers a perfect way to end the day**. (源自2025.1.11北京范文) |
| **困难事物** (Difficult thing)     | **困难描述**: challenging, nervous, heart races, make mistakes <br> **原因/重要性**: boost confidence, good communication, express ideas | **My heart races**, and I worry about forgetting my words or **making mistakes**. (源自2025.2.15上海范文) <br> Good communication can **boost confidence** and help people share their thoughts. (源自2025.2.15上海范文) |
| **人际友谊** (How to make friends) | **观点**: look after each other, in good and bad times <br> **方法**: join clubs, be a good listener, offer to help, give a compliment <br> **升华**: True friendship is rare, treasure it | **Last but not least**, be kind and helpful. (源自2025.4.12济南范文) <br> **True friendship is rare, so treasure it when you find it!** (源自2025.4.12济南范文) |
| **居住环境** (Living environment)  | **问题**: noisy, disorganized, parked in wrong places <br> **建议**: create designated parking spaces, more green spaces <br> **重要性**: essential for well-being, provide fresh air | This **disorganized** parking makes it difficult for **pedestrians** to walk safely. (源自2025.4.26北京范文) <br> **Green areas are essential for our health and well-being.** (源自2025.4.26北京范文) |
| **敬佩的人** (A person you admire) | **品质**: incredibly kind, thinking of others, never expects thanks, shares generously <br> **影响**: taught me that happiness comes from helping, brightened the community | **His motto seems to be: "If I have it and you need it, let's share!"** (源自2025.7.19北京范文) <br> **I truly admire his big heart**, and I want to be more like him. (源自2025.7.19北京范文) |
| **兴趣爱好** (Painting/Music)      | **好处**: express thoughts and feelings, escape from stress, a rewarding activity <br> **描述音乐**: gentle, quiet, focus better, distract me | Painting is a great way to **express my thoughts and feelings**. (源自2025.2.23南京范文) <br> Soft piano music **never distracts me while I study**. (源自2025.8.16北京范文) <br> **It is a wonderful idea to** give a handmade painting as a gift. (源自2025.2.23南京范文) |
| **读书** (Reading)                 | **类型**: detective and comedy books <br> **情节描述**: unexpected, sneaking, didn't panic, tricked <br> **感受**: funny and clever, makes me laugh | **The plot is interesting because it's unexpected.** (源自2025.3.8北京范文) <br> The thieves were **tricked by a child**. (源自2025.3.8北京范文) |

### **Part 3. 故事类 (Story) —— 按叙事结构 + 常用元素归类**

故事写作的关键是“一波三折”和“细节描写”。我们把素材放进叙事流程里，让故事立刻生动起来。

| 结构块       | 功能                                   | 典型素材（标注出素材来源）                                   |
| :----------- | :------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------- |
| **开头承接** | 承接首句，设置背景，引入人物状态。     | **句型**: It was... when...; I was... when suddenly... <br> **时态**: 过去进行时 (was/were doing)  <br> **例句**: **I was waiting for the bus when I saw** a tiger running towards me. (源自Page 9) <br> **例句**: **He was reading a book when** an apple fell on his head. (源自Page 11范文分析-2) |
| **发展推进** | 描述一系列动作，使用连接词使故事流畅。 | **时间词**: first, then, later, **a few days later**, finally, **in the end** (源自Page 9) <br> **动作副词**: **instantly**, quickly, slowly, carefully <br> **例句**: **Instantly, she picked up** the letter and started to read it. (源自Page 10范文分析-1) <br> **例句**: **A few days later, he went back** to that cave with his friends. (源自Page 9) |
| **高潮转折** | 故事发生意外转折，情绪达到顶点。       | **转折词**: but, however, suddenly, to one's surprise <br> **情绪词**: **panicked**, astonished, amazed, exhausted <br> **评论副词**: **unfortunately**, luckily, fortunately <br> **例句**: **Suddenly, I heard a weak cry.** A boy was lying under the deck! (源自Page 12范文2) <br> **例句**: **To their surprise, it was a cute little rabbit!** (源自Page 15范文1) |
| **结尾收束** | 总结结局，点明意义或留下回味。         | **结尾句**: That day, we became friends...; It was an incredible encounter... <br> **升华**: ...became a wonderful memory...; ...added a splash of color to their ordinary lives. <br> **例句**: **That day at the beach became a wonderful memory** for Jesse and her friend. (源自Page 13范文1) <br> **例句**: **This adventure had added a splash of color to their ordinary lives.** (源自Page 13范文2) |

### **📚 通用素材库 (Tool Box)**

无论写什么，这些“万能”词汇都能帮你提升语言质量。

| 类型                    | 典型素材                                                     |
| :---------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------- |
| **亮点时间/逻辑连接词** | First, Then, Later, **A few days later**, Finally, **In the end**, **However**, **Suddenly**, **Unfortunately**, **Luckily**, **As soon as** |
| **高级动作副词**        | **Instantly** (立刻), **Quickly** (快速地), **Slowly** (缓慢地), **Carefully** (小心地), **Angrily** (生气地), **Gracefully** (优雅地), **Leisurely** (悠闲地) |
| **情绪形容词**          | **Excited** (兴奋的), **Amazed** (惊奇的), **Panicked** (恐慌的), **Relieved** (松了一口气的), **Astonished** (震惊的), **Exhausted** (筋疲力尽的), **Delighted** (高兴的) |
| **万能动词短语**        | **take a leisurely walk** (悠闲地散步), **have a picnic** (野餐), **boost confidence** (提升自信), **offer to help** (主动帮忙), **give a compliment** (给予赞美), **escape from stress** (逃离压力), **unwind** (放松) |
| **高分句型结构**        | **定语从句** (...which/that/who...) <br> **It allows me to...** <br> **It is a wonderful idea to...** <br> **I believe that...** <br> **The reason why...is that...** |
